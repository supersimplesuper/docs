# Policy attestations

Ensure every employee has read and agreed to your company policies before they start. Upload policies like a Code of Conduct, WHS policy or privacy policy and present them as part of the onboarding flow. Employees review each policy, acknowledge it with a single click and can download a copy for their own records.

## Features

* Upload policies in PDF or Word format and manage them via a custom embed.
* Present multiple policies in a single onboarding session with configurable ordering.
* Employees acknowledge each policy with an agree button — no signature required.
* Employees can download a copy of each policy to keep for their records.
